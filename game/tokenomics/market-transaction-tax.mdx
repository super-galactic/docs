UAP includes a market level transaction tax that applies to **secondary market trading only**.

This mechanism is completely separate from in game spending, progression systems, and gameplay related burn mechanics.

## Scope of the Transaction Tax

The market transaction tax applies exclusively to **sell transactions** involving UAP.

- **0% tax on buys**
- **10% tax on sells**

This design applies no tax on acquisition while enforcing a defined rule on sell side transactions.

---

## Sell Tax Overview

When UAP is sold on supported markets:

- A **10% sell tax** is applied
- The tax is enforced at the smart contract level
- The mechanism is transparent and verifiable on chain

<Note>
  The sell tax does not apply to in game usage, upgrades, breeding, or progression related actions.
</Note>

---

## Purpose of the Market Transaction Tax

The market transaction tax exists to provide **economic flexibility and long term stability**.

Sell tax proceeds may be used, when required, to:

- Support overall ecosystem balance
- Reinforce supply control through discretionary burns
- Enable community aligned initiatives

The 10% sell tax rate is fixed. The allocation or use of collected sell tax proceeds may vary based on ecosystem needs, such as discretionary burns or community aligned initiatives.

---

## Separation from Gameplay Mechanics

Market transaction taxes operate independently from in game economic systems.

- Gameplay spending follows automated burn and treasury mechanisms
- Rewards and progression are governed by the Game Economy
- Market taxes apply only to external trading activity

This separation ensures clarity between **trading behavior** and **gameplay progression**.

## Transparency and Enforcement

The market transaction tax:

- Is enforced automatically by smart contracts
- Cannot be bypassed on sell transactions
- Is publicly visible and auditable on chain

Any adjustments to sell tax usage or parameters, if ever required, would be communicated transparently.

---

## Design Principles

The market transaction tax follows these principles:

- No friction on entry
- Predictable exit rules
- Clear separation from gameplay economics
- Flexibility without inflation

This ensures market rules remain understandable while preserving long term ecosystem health.
