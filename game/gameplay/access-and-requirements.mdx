This page defines the network, wallet, and asset requirements for progression and Play to Earn features.

### Network

Super Galactic’s progression and Play to Earn settlement operates on **BNB Chain**.

### Wallet

A wallet is required only for modes and actions that involve ownership, progression, or UAP transactions.

Supported wallets:

- MetaMask (recommended)
- Coinbase Wallet
- WalletConnect compatible wallets

<Note>
  Demo gameplay remains wallet free.
</Note>

### Play to Earn Requirements

Play to Earn is available through **PvE missions only**.

To access Play to Earn PvE and progression features:

- Connect a supported wallet
- Own at least one **Genesis character NFT**

Genesis characters can be minted or acquired through the [Super Galactic Hub](https://app.ufogaming.io/) marketplace.

### Starter Weapon Pack

Players who enter Play to Earn PvE gameplay using a Genesis Origin UFOep receive a starter weapon pack.

Starter weapon rules:

- Includes one melee weapon and one ranged weapon
- Non-NFT
- Non-upgradeable

Weapon NFTs and advanced equipment are optional.

See NFTs & Assets → [Lootbox Pricing](https://supergalactic.mintlify.app/game/nfts-assets/minting-and-pricing#lootbox-pricing).
