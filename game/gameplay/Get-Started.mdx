Super Galactic is designed to be easy to start playing while supporting deeper progression over time. Players can enter the game immediately and decide later how deeply they want to engage with progression, ownership, and earning systems.

This section explains how to access the game, choose a platform, configure basic settings, and begin playing.

### Controls and Input

Super Galactic supports multiple input methods.

Players can use:

- Mouse and keyboard
- Xbox compatible controllers

Input method selection does not affect gameplay mechanics, matchmaking, or progression. Players can choose the control method that best suits their playstyle.

### Select a Server Region

Before entering gameplay, players must select a server region.

Choosing the correct region is important for:

- Low latency combat
- Stable multiplayer performance
- Playing with friends and teams

<Tip>
  Players who want to match or form parties together must be connected to the same server region.
</Tip>

### Enter the Game

Once a platform and server region are selected, players can enter gameplay immediately.

New players can:

- Play demo modes to learn mechanics and controls
- Enter PvE or PvP modes based on availability
- Explore combat, movement, and weapons

No wallet connection or asset ownership is required to access demo gameplay.

### Accessing Progression and Play to Earn

Players who want to unlock progression and Play to Earn PvE gameplay must connect a wallet and own a **Genesis Origin UFOep NFT**.

Once connected:

- Owned characters become available in game
- Progression tracking is enabled
- Play to Earn PvE missions can be accessed

A single Genesis NFT is sufficient to unlock progression and earning eligibility.

### Tutorials and Learning

Super Galactic includes optional tutorials designed to help players learn core mechanics.

Players can:

- Complete tutorials before entering gameplay
- Skip tutorials and learn through active play

Tutorials are designed to support both new and experienced players.

### Getting Deeper Over Time

Super Galactic does not require immediate commitment.

Players can:

- Play casually in demo and multiplayer modes
- Gradually explore progression systems
- Decide when or if they want to engage with ownership and Play to Earn

This flexible onboarding allows players to move at their own pace without pressure.
