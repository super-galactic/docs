Super Galactic is a community-driven Play to Earn game built around real gameplay first, with ownership and rewards designed to reinforce long-term engagement rather than short-term extraction. 

The game is designed to be playable immediately, without requiring a wallet or upfront asset purchase. Players can experience the full core gameplay, combat systems, and progression flow before engaging with ownership mechanics. This approach allows new players to understand the game on its own terms, while ownership and earning unlock naturally over time through participation rather than forced entry.

<Info>
  By separating access from ownership, Super Galactic lowers onboarding friction and enables players to introduce friends and family to the game through real gameplay, not speculation.
</Info>

The project has been in active development for over four years, spanning multiple market cycles. Building began during the 2021 bull market and continued through the subsequent bear market, with uninterrupted development, iterative testing, and continuous feedback from the community across alpha and beta phases. This process shaped Super Galactic's focus on skill-based gameplay, sustainable progression, and systems designed to endure beyond launch cycles.

Rather than treating tokens and NFTs as speculative entry points, Super Galactic integrates them as functional components of gameplay. Progression, upgrades, and long-term asset development are earned through active participation, not passive mechanics. Rewards are tied to performance, commitment, and mastery.

Super Galactic operates as a live ecosystem. The custom-built Super Galactic Hub is already live, supporting asset management, progression visibility, and on-chain ownership, while the game itself is playable in open beta. The UAP token is designed to activate utility across these systems, completing a closed-loop economy that has been tested before being introduced at scale.

This documentation outlines how Super Galactic is structured, how its economy functions, and how its design choices reflect a new generation of Play to Earn games built for longevity and player engagement.
